We Are A Non-profit Organization 

Tec-Uganda is a non-profit Uganda based organisation with a goal of building and supporting an attitude of self-sustaining homesteads in marginalised communities of Uganda through health promotion, leadership development, building creative, innovative mindset and positive attitudes towards life. We further train practical, marketable skills at homestead level.

MISSION

To build a community of self-sustaining independent individuals and homesteads through a consortium of privilleged professionals across the devide. To skill, motivate and empower the socio-economic status of young people and marginalised communities in Uganda.

CORE VALUES 

Team work, Selflessness, Leadership , Boldness and humility, Integrity , Innovation, creativity and quality

VISION
Self sustaining homesteads
